Three Horizontal Overview Ideas

These are three different ways to keep the overview horizontal and easier to scan. I kept the same pinned-model language and model colors so you can compare the layout, not the data language.

Thermal v1

1. Equal-width Panels

Keep the three pinned models as full cards in one row, but flatten the content into wider, calmer sections so the eye moves left to right instead of top to bottom.

balanced / familiar

2. Horizontal Lanes

Each model becomes one long lane. The rail color stays on the left, while coverage and metrics stretch out in a single horizontal line, which is much less mind-numbing to scan.

most horizontal

3. Comparison First

Make the metric comparison the star, then keep the pinned-model summaries compact underneath. This is the least card-heavy version and the easiest to compare at a glance.
